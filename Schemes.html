Central Schemes

eNAM

National Agriculture Market
This scheme is an online platform that connects farmers with buyers across the country and provides them with better market access and price realization.

PMFBY

Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana
This scheme aims to provide affordable crop insurance to farmers against crop loss due to natural calamities, pests, and diseases.

RKVY

Rashtriya Krishi Vikas Yojana
This scheme provides financial assistance to states and union territories for the implementation of agriculture-related projects and activities.

PM-KMY

Pradhan Mantri Kisan Maan Dhan Yojana
This scheme is a voluntary and contributory pension scheme for farmers in the age group of 18 to 40 years, with a minimum pension of Rs. 3,000 per month on attaining the age of 60 years.

PKVY

Paramparagat Krishi Vikas Yojana
This scheme promotes organic farming and sustainable agriculture practices through the adoption of traditional farming techniques.

State Schemes

RBS

Rythu Bandhu Scheme
The government provides financial assistance to farmers for the purchase of inputs like seeds,fertilizers,pesticides, and labour costs for cultivation of crops. Currently,the government provides Rs. 10,000 per acre per year to farmers.

RB

Rythu Bima Scheme
This is an insurance scheme for farmers. Under this scheme, farmers are provided with financial assistance in case of crop damage due to natural calamities, pests, or diseases.

ADSS

Annadata Sukhibhava Scheme
This scheme provides financial assistance to farmers who own land up to 5 acres. Under this scheme, eligible farmers are given Rs. 10,000 per year to support their farming activities.

RRS

Rythu Raksha Scheme
This scheme provides crop insurance to farmers in case of crop failure due to natural calamities or other reasons. Under this scheme, eligible farmers are given Rs. 10,000 per acre per year.

YSRRBS

YSR Rythu Bharosa Scheme
This scheme aims to provide financial assistance to farmers for crop investment, purchase of inputs, and post-harvest activities. Under this scheme, eligible farmers are given Rs. 13,500 per year.
