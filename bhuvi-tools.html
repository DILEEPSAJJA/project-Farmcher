Nitrogen sensors

Agriculture

Sensing technology applied to agriculture is based on the typical light reflectance curve for vegetation. N-Sensor measures light reflectance at specific wave bands related to the crop's chlorophyll content and biomass. It calculates the actual N-uptake of the crop

Driverless Tractors

Agriculture

A driverless tractor is an autonomous farm vehicle that delivers a high tractive effort at slow speeds for the purposes of tillage and other agricultural tasks. It is considered driverless because it operates without the presence of a human inside the tractor itself.

Robotic Milking

Rancher

Milking robots are automatic milking systems where milking is performed without direct participation of an operator. The cows are milked throughout the day, including 1 or 2 h needed for cleaning the equipme nt. An average number of milkings per day is 2.5–3. The result is by 10–15% higher production.
